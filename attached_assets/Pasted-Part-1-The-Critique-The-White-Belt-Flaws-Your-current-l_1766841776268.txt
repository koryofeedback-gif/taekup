Part 2: The Remastered "Home Dojo" (The Black Belt Upgrade)
We will keep the same habits, but we will rename them and add flavor text. This changes the psychology from "I have to do this" to "I am leveling up."

The 4 Pillars of the Home Dojo
1. The Tatami Check (Orderliness)

Old Name: Make Bed.

The Logic: A chaotic room equals a chaotic mind.

App Text: "Secure the Perimeter." (Did you make your bed and clear your floor?)

2. Armor Maintenance (Health)

Old Name: Brush Teeth.

The Logic: Your body is your weapon and your armor. You must polish it.

App Text: "Polish the Armor." (Teeth brushed, face washed, uniform folded?)

3. The Code of Honor (Character)

Old Name: Show Respect.

The Logic: Respect must be actionable.

App Text: "The First Command." (Did you listen the first time you were asked, without arguing?)

4. Service to the Tribe (Chores)

Old Name: Help with Chores.

The Logic: A warrior serves others.

App Text: "Serve the Tribe." (Did you complete one task to help the family without being asked?)

Part 4: The UX Strategy (Making it Addictive)
To make this "Perfect," you need one visual element: The Streak Flame.

If a child hits 60/60 XP for 3 days in a row, show a Blue Flame icon next to their profile.

If they hit 7 days, it turns into a Golden Dragon.
