## 3. Core Game Mechanics (The "Sticky" Factors)

### I. The "Gacha" Summoning System
We replace the boring "Shop" with an addictive **Random Number Generator (RNG)** mechanism.
* **Input:** 1,000 XP (earned in class) = 1 Summon Scroll.
* **Output:** Students roll for Common (50%), Rare (35%), Epic (14%), or **Legendary (1%)** items.
* **Pity System:** If a student rolls 10 times with no good loot, the system guarantees a Rare item to prevent quitting (Bad Luck Protection).

### II. The "Limit Breaker" (Real-World Integration)
*The feature that makes Instructors love us.*
* **The Conflict:** A White Belt student might roll a "Legendary Dragon Spirit" (Power: 5,000).
* **The Lock:** The game caps their power at 50 because their physical body (White Belt) is too weak.
* **The Resolution:** The app explicitly tells them: *"Pass your Yellow Belt grading to unlock this spirit's full power."*
* **Result:** The game drives the student to pay for grading and stay in class.

### III. Seasonal Region Locking
* **Longevity:** The game is not linear. It is episodic.
* **White Belts** play in Season 1 (Bamboo Forest).
* **Green Belts** unlock Season 2 (Toxic Swamp).
* **Black Belts** unlock Season 10 (Cosmic Arena).
* *Why:* This creates mystery. A beginner cannot access high-level content, keeping the game fresh for years.
