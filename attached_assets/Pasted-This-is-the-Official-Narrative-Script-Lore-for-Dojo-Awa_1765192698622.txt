## Why This Story Works for Business?

1.  **The "Egg" Hook (Tamagotchi Effect):**
    * In the first month (White Belt), the child *cannot* leave the club because they want to see what is inside the egg. Curiosity drives retention.

2.  **The "Cliffhangers" (Limit Breakers):**
    * At the end of every belt, the story explicitly **stops**. The student is stuck at a gate. The *only* key is the instructor's approval (grading). This turns the instructor into the "Hero Maker."

3.  **The Visual Progression:**
    * A White Belt looks like a peasant (Cloth gear).
    * A Red Belt looks like a Warlord (Fire gear).
    * A Black Belt looks like a God (Cosmic gear).
    * This visual hierarchy makes students desperate to upgrade their status.

4.  **Flexible Lore:**
    * This story works for **Taekwondo** (Kicks/Speed).
    * It works for **BJJ** (Grappling/Control).
    * It works for **Karate** (Strikes/Power).
    * You just change the names of the "Regions," but the structure (Forest -> Mountain -> Fire -> Space) remains the same.

This is the script. It provides the emotional context for every push-up, every grading fee, and every login.
